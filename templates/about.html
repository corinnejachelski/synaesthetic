About

Synaesthetic was solely created by Corinne Jachelski as a capstone project for Hackbright Academy's Software Engineering Intensive Program. Hackbright is an engineering school for women dedicated to the mission of fostering more diversity in the technology industry, and is consistently ranked as a Best Bootcamp by Course Report.

Synaesthetic is a Spotify app for visualizing user-specific data, based on the idea of “seeing sound” (synesthesia) and loosely inspired by Corinne's excitement around her annual Spotify Unwrapped data. Utilizing OAuth and the Spotify Web API, users can explore their listening patterns and relationships among their top artists and genres via an interactive dashboard of stats and charts made with D3.js and Charts.js (aesthetic).
